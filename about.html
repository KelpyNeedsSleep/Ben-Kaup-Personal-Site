About Me

Hello! My name is Benjamin E. Kaup, and I'm passionate about games, philosophy, poetry, and computer science. I grew up in Maryland and graduated early from high school to pursue my education at Clark University.

Outside of work and studies, I have a deep love for nature, people, and philosophy. I enjoy capturing the beauty of the outdoors and people around me to find inspiration for my poetry in those experiences.

Professionally, I have a wide array of interests spanning game development, artificial intelligence, and creative writing. This site serves as a portfolio where I can share my work and my thoughts with the world. Feel free to explore my poetry or learn more about my background.
